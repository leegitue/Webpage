About the Event

The PU Festival and Student Day is a yearly event that celebrates the achievements, talents, and vibrant spirit of Panpacific University students. It features performances, contests, exhibits, and cultural showcases that unite students, faculty, and staff in a joyful and collaborative atmosphere.

This celebration highlights not only academic excellence but also creativity, leadership, and teamwork — reflecting the true identity of every PU student.
